    In this season ignite your senses and spend a rejuvenating holiday with your better half. With one prime destination to pamper and enkindle from the green meadows of Munnar to the colonial streets of Cochin, Kerala promises to give you a detachable one-on-one time all day long. If you are looking for a short wonderfull getaway, this is the right choice especially made for you.
    In our 03/04 days Kerala Alleppey tour you can feel renewed and refreshed with our fine selection of attractions you will see. From mesmerizing waterfalls like Cheyyara, Valara, and Attakadu, you will be dosed with Spice Plantation, Kodanadu Elephant Care Centre, Christ Church, Eravikulam National Park and Madupetty Dam.     Itinerary

    Day1------Arrive Cochin - Munnar

    Upon arrival at Cochin, you will meet our representative, later you will be followed by a drive to Munnar. En-routing to Munnar, you can witness beautiful waterfalls like Cheyyara, Valara, and Attakadu along with spice plantation. You will also stop by Kodanadu Elephant Care Centre. Later, with an overnight stay in Munnar.

    Day2------Munnar

    After breakfast, you can enjoy a full day of sightseeing in and around Munnar. Enjoy panoramic views of Anamudi, the highest peak of South India at 2695 m, which is visible from Munnar. Visit the Christ Church and proceed for wildlife watching at Eravikulam National Park (16 km). After that, you will continue your tour to Rajamalai Hills lying adjacent to the park and stop by at Madupetty Dam which is a famous picnic spot for boating and horse riding. Later, you will be transferred to the hotel for an overnight stay.

    Day3------Munnar

    You can enjoy a free day touring on your own or you can pay a visit to the beautiful tea plantation. Later, follow with an overnight stay at the selected hotel.

    Day4------Munnar

    After breakfast at the hotel, you will be driven back to Cochin. On arrival, at Cochin International Airport, you will be connecting flights for your onward destination.
