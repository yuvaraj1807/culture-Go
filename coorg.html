    Sightseeing

    If you love the chirping of bird and want to witness the wilderness in the midst of verdant environs, then Coorg is the place for you. The serenity of the surroundings and the Mother Nature showing its beauty at its best altogether form a setting no less than an empyrean for nature lovers. This 04 Nights and 05 Days itinerary to Coorg and Kabini is comprehensively dedicated to the travellers having an inclination towards bird watching and witnessing wildlife and nature's bounty. The major highlights of this trip are basking in the midst of rich greenery of Coorg, enjoying jungle safari at Kabini National Park, exploring local tribes in Kabini and bird watching in Coorg. It is a notable fact that Coorg boasts around 1/4th of bird species in the country. The tour makes the tourists break the monotony of life, enjoy serenity and pamper the senses in a consummate way.

    Itinerary

    Day1------Bangalore - Coorg

    On arrival at Bangalore railway station/airport, meet our representative who will be there for your welcome. Thereafter, our representative will take you to Coorg by road, which is about five hour journey. On reaching Coorg, we will check in at the hotel. The evening time is free at leisure. During your free time, you can stay back in your spacious room and rest or enjoy the modern amenities of the hotel. An overnight stay in the hotel. Coorg is traveller's delight and famous for its bulk coffee plantation, waterfalls and soothing weather. The place is one of the major tourist attractions in the Southern India that attract thousands of visitors from around the world.

    Day2------Bangalore

    In the morning after breakfast, we will proceed for Bangalore's local sightseeing tour. In our day tour, we will visit some of the major attractions of the city and have a lifetime experience. Some of the places we will be having in our Bangalore day tour include Vidhan Sabha, Lal Bagh Botanical Garden, a government edifice built in Neo Dravidian style, Tipu's Palace, Bangalore Palace, etc. later in the evening , we will return back to the hotel for an overnight stay. Bangalore is the capital of Karnataka state of India. Situated in southern India on the Deccan Plateau, at an altitude of about 900 m (3,000 ft) above sea level, the city is acknowledged for its pleasant climate all over the year.

    Day3------Coorg - Kabini

    Today after having breakfast in the hotel, we will check out and drive to Kabini, which is about two and a half hour journey. On arrival, check in at your pre-booked hotel. Afterwards, get ready to explore the major attractions of Kabini such as Kabini River, Kabini Dam, Nagarhole National Park, Irpu Falls, Rameswara Temple, etc. The seightseeing tour of Kabini required a complete day where in you can explore most of its tourist places. After these places throughout the day, we will return back to our hotel in the late evening and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay. Kabini is one of the most preferred travel destinations among the families, couples and travel lovers. Located near Bangalore, the place is blessed with rich forest cover, waterfalls and valleys. It also contains an abundance of vegetation.

    Day4------Kabini

    After breakfast in the morning, we will be visiting a tribal village to explore the lifestyle of locals. On reaching the village, we will interact with locals to learn more about their unique culture and lifestyle. After having lunch with locals in the village only, we will head for Elephant Jungle safari to explore the real natural beauty of the place. Later, in the evening we will head back to the hotel for a stress free overnight stay.

    Day5----------Kumarakom - Cochin Departure

    On the last day of our tour, we will have the breakfast and afterwards, check out from the hotel to return back to Bangalore. On arrival, get direct transfer to the airport/railway station to catch flight or board train for onward destination.
