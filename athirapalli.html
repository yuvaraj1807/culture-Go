    Itinerary

    Day1------Arrive Cochin - Athirapally

    On arrival at Cochin, meet the representatives who will a take you on a drive to Athirapally to get the glimpse of the mighty fall of Athirapally & Vazhachal. After a short visit, check-in the hotel for overnight stay.

    Day2------Athirappilly

    Post breakfast, rest of the day will be free for leisure. You can either revisit the waterfalls or visit attractions like Peringalkuthu Dam, Charpa Falls, Silver Storm Water Theme Park, etc

    Day3------Athirappilly

    After breakfast, you will embark on a drive to Alleppey to enjoy the iconic backwater cruise ride. Enjoy the backwaters of Kerala by getting a glimpse of the village life at close quarters; a unique and unforgettable experience. Dinner, and overnight stay at the Alleppey hotel.

    Day4------Alleppey - Kumarakom

    After breakfast drive to Kumarakom. Check into a resort at the bank of the Vembanad Lake. Rest of the day will be free for leisure. Overnight stay at the Kumarakom hotel.

    Day5------Kumarakom - Cochin Departure

    Post breakfast, you will drive back to Cochin to take embark on your journey onwards.
