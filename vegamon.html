    Itinerary

    Day1------Arrive Cochin – Vagamon

    Upon arrival at Cochin, you will be meeting our representatives followed by a drive to Vagamon. On arrival at Vagamon, you can check-in at selected hotel followed by an overnight stay. "Vagamon was discovered by the British who found the place ideal for tea plantations and they were followed by Christian missionaries who built their abode of service at Kurisumala in Vagamon.

    Day2------Vagamon

    After breakfast at the hotel, you can continue to tour around Vagamon - a sleepy town with its scenic valleys and mists, beautiful green meadows, enchanting pine forests, tea estates, waterfalls with the overall greenery is really striking. Vagamon is also home to a large variety of flowers and orchids. The landscape is mostly hilly bald with green meadows. After sightseeing, you will be spending an overnight stay at selected hotel in Vagamon.

    Day3------Vagamon - Alleppey

    After breakfast at the hotel, you will be driven to Alleppey. Enjoy the magic of backwater by going on a Houseboat (Kettuvallam) cruise across the backwaters. Spend the next 22 hours in your floating villa. Enjoy the backwaters of Kerala by getting a glimpse of the village life at close quarters; a unique and unforgettable experience. Enjoy the sunset and stay overnight at the houseboat, all inclusive of breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

    Day4------Alleppey - Kumarakom

    After breakfast, drive to Kumarakom. Once here you can check into a resort at the bank of the Vembanand Lake where you will be amazed and you will forget everything except the simple enjoyment of life. You can rest for the day followed by an overnight at the resort.

    Day5------Kumarakom - Cochin Departure

    After breakfast at the hotel, you will be driven back to Cochin. On arrival at Cochin, you will be dropped at the airport to connect flight for your onward destination.
