    Itinerary

    Day1------COIMBATORE / OOTY

    Once you arrive at Coimbatore / Madurai you’ll be well assisted by our executive from where you will proceed to Ooty, the famed "Queen of Hills" in the Nilgiris of South India. Upon your arrival in Ooty, you’ll be checked into the hotel for overnight stay

    Day2------Ooty-coonoor

    A new day begins packed with scrummy breakfast after which you head to Ooty Railway Station to relish the serene view through the joyous toy train ride from Ooty to Coonoor (Direct Payment Basis). As you reach Coonoor you can pave your way to sight the Lamb Rock, Dolphin's Nose and later on get back to Ooty and on the way visit Rose garden, Botanical garden & Ooty Lake. Overnight stay in Ooty.

    Day3------OOTY / KODAIKANAL

    The plan for today is to proceed to Kodaikanal which is among the most popular hill station in this southern state. You will be left surprised as the place is jam-packed with green trees swaying here and there, lushful plantations, to the aura with natural boundaries making up the best view one can imagine. As you arrive, check-in at your hotel and spend some time lazing around and stay overnight.

    Day4------KODAIKANAL

    This morning can be different from others as you start your day with a healthy breakfast and camera in bag to capture the Kodaikanal Pillar rocks, Kodai lake, & Coaker's walk. Overnight stay in Kodaikanal.

    Day5------KODAIKANAL / COIMBATORE

    After breakfast, head for your train/flight on time and don't forget to carry your bag full of memories back home.
