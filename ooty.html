    Sightseeing

    Situated deep in the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty is among the key tourist destinations in Southern India. Right from the Botanical Garden to the Ooty Lake the city has so much to gift to the travelers. Our Ooty Hill Tour Package makes you grab all these gifts with both hands. We take you to every popular destination of Ooty hill stations, be it the St. Stephen’s Church or the Wax World Museum. Further, we also take you to Ooty’s nearby attraction, Coonoor, where scores of natural wonders wait to welcome you. Thus, grab our Ooty holiday package and enjoy an unmatched travel experience.

    Itinerary

    Day1------COIMBATORE / OOTY

    Early morning drive to Ooty from Coimbatore and on arrival check into a pre-booked hotel. In the afternoon, half day sightseeing tour of Ooty, which includes Botanical Garden, Ooty Boat House (Ooty Lake),Rose Garden, St. Stephen's Church and Wax World Museum. Evening spent in leisure. Dinner and overnight stay in the hotel.

    Day2------Ooty-coonoor-Ooty

    In the morning after breakfast, proceed for sightseeing tour of Conoor. The tour will include a visit to Sims Park and Dolphin’s Nose Point. In the evening drive back to Ooty and spend rest of the time at leisure. Overnight stay at a hotel in Ooty.

    Day3------KODAIKANAL / COIMBATORE

    After breakfast, head for your train/flight on time and don't forget to carry your bag full of memories back home.
